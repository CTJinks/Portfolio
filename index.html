Welcome...

If you've come here looking for an upcoming developer with a drive to learn and succeed, then this is the place for you. My name is Christian Jinks and I am currently enrolled in the Software Development course at Eleven Fifty Academy. After travelling the country participating as a performer in Drum Corps International and 3 wonderful years Bartending and Serving I decided to attend the academy to expand my my knowledge in this growing industry and I have had no regrets since.

Outside Interests

Drum Corps and WGI

Growing up in Avon, Indiana and participating in the Avon Marching Black and Gold exposed me to what have so far been one of the most defining experiences of my life. After high school I spent 4 amazing years travelling the country participating in different Drum Corps and WGI Independent World Class Drumline drumline. Through these experiences I have been introduced to the true meanings of hard work, dedication, and team work.

Hiking and Travel

After getting a taste of travelling with Drum Corps and WGI, I knew it was something I wanted to incorporate into my life. In the summer of 2018 I successfully did a week long trek in the Rocky Mountains. This opportunity to connect with nature helped teach me the importance of exploration and self-reflection. I have future aspirations to do multi-week treks in both The Appalachian Trail and The Pacific Crest Trail.

Music

To say that music has been always been a big part of my life is an understatement. From my days when I was a kid and my mom would play classic Disney songs on the piano, to my experiences in Performance Arts, to my current passion for playing guitar music has been a corner-stone of how I have been shaped as a person. I have innumerable unforgettable experiences through music including performing next to Madonna and Cee Lo Green in the Super Bowl XLVI Half-Time Show and i know that there will be more to come.
